Officers

President

Juan Carlos Castillo

Universidad de Chile, Chile

Professor at the Department of Sociology, Universidad de Chile, and Director of Research and Publications at the Faculty of Social Sciences. His work focuses on distributive justice, citizenship education, social cohesion, quantitative methods, structural equation modeling, multilevel analysis, and open science.

Distributive justice Citizenship education Social cohesion Open science Quantitative methods

Lisa Slattery Walker

University of North Carolina at Charlotte, USA

Professor of Sociology at UNC Charlotte. Her research focuses on small group interaction, nonverbal behaviors, identity, emotions, gender, expectations, and status processes.

Small group interaction Identity Emotions Gender Status processes

Race and identity Methodology Urban sociology Demography Research methods

Karla Henríquez Ojeda

Activism Social movements Youth Emotions Latin America
