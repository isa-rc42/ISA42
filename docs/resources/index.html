Publications

Recent books, special journal issues, journals, and significant articles relevant to sociological social psychology.

Social Psychology Quarterly
American Sociological Association / SAGE · Journal
Core journal for sociological social psychology. It publishes theoretical and empirical work on the link between individuals, groups, institutions, and social structure.
Language: English · Access: Mixed access / subscription · Verified
journal sociological social psychology interaction self and society social structure

Open resource →

Handbook of Social Psychology
John DeLamater & Amanda Ward, eds. / Springer · Handbook
Broad handbook covering major theoretical perspectives and topics in social psychology, including interactionist, identity, social exchange, and social structure approaches.
Language: English · Access: Subscription / purchase · Verified
handbook theory identity interactionism social exchange social structure

Open resource →

Handbook of the Social Psychology of Inequality
Jane D. McLeod, Edward J. Lawler & Michael Schwalbe, eds. / Springer · Handbook
Major reference on how social psychological processes mediate, reproduce, and contest inequality. Strong fit for RC42 themes on status, power, identity, stigma, and justice.
Language: English · Access: Subscription / purchase · Verified
inequality status power stigma justice intersectionality handbook

Open resource →

Contemporary Social Psychological Theories
Peter J. Burke, ed. / Stanford University Press · Book
Presents major theory programs in sociological social psychology, including identity, exchange, affect control, expectation states, and related approaches.
Language: English · Access: Book / purchase; excerpt available · Verified
theory identity theory exchange theory affect control theory expectation states symbolic interactionism

Open resource →

Call for Papers: Special Issue on Social Status
Social Psychology Quarterly · Call for papers / special issue
Special issue call focused on how social status is determined and how it affects individuals, groups, and society. Useful for highlighting current agendas in status, hierarchy, and inequality.
Language: English · Access: Open web page · Verified
status inequality hierarchy call for papers journal

Open resource →

Emotions and Society
Bristol University Press · Journal
Interdisciplinary journal focused on emotions as social, cultural, political, and relational phenomena. Relevant for sociology of emotions and affective dimensions of social life.
Language: English · Access: Mixed access / subscription · Verified
emotions affect culture politics journal

Open resource →

On the Justice of Earnings: A New Specification of the Justice Evaluation Function
Guillermina Jasso / American Journal of Sociology · Journal article
Classic article in justice evaluation theory. Relevant for distributive justice, inequality, social comparison, and mathematical sociology in social psychology.
Language: English · Access: Subscription / institutional access · Verified
justice inequality distributive justice social comparison classic article

Open resource →

Social Identity: Sociological and Social Psychological Perspectives
Social Psychology Quarterly / JSTOR · Special issue
Classic special issue connecting social identity theory with sociological and social psychological perspectives. Useful for identity, group processes, and boundary-making themes.
Language: English · Access: JSTOR / institutional access · Verified
social identity identity group processes special issue classic

Language: English · Access: Open data for non-commercial research · Verified
values attitudes trust religion gender comparative research survey data
